title: 'Rust Clippy announces that it will require age verification software for users residing in California'
date: 2026-04-01T18:48:00+02:00
draft: false
---

Rust [Clippy](https://github.com/rust-lang/rust-clippy), [Rust](https://rust-lang.org)'s official linter has announced that age verification software will be included as soon as Rust 1.97.0. This comes with no surprise as Rust has historically standed with a firm policy of user safety.

Head developers at Rust have pointed out that while some might find the announcement uncomfortable, user safety and kyc (know-your-customer) guidelines are vital for keeping a safe environment, and that California's _Age-appropiate Design Code_ act is a huge first step for online safety.

> We cannot allow minors, specially children to roam around unsupervised on the internet, and especially around programming and tech-related websites.
> I think that this initiative taken by California's government will help take the burden off the parents and AI, and into our hands.
> I like being identified.
>
> Alejandra González, Clippy team member.

Other Rust developers liked the "relatability" aspect that it brings.

> I feel like if we are all monitored, it really brings us all together. We are in this together.
>
> Anonymous

No exact release date has been announced as of now, but it seems that Age-Verification is coming to the Rust world and is coming strong.
